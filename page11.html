Training During Covid 19

Each Child MUST be accompanied By a PARENT for the whole session in case of illness or if First Aid has to be administered

Parents must adhere to the 2m social distance rule on the side lines and from other people BUT must remain in sight of their children at ALL times .

Please enter and exit the site from the car park.  Keep to the left of the entrance way to maintain social distance.

There is to be NO games as NO contact is allowed.
Individual skills and fitness training only.
Each child must have or be provided with their own ball.

All COACHES & CHILDREN must use hand sanitizer when they arrive and when they leave .
All Equipment MUST be cleaned before starting and at the end of each session .


The containers are NOT to be Accessed at anytime . So each coach must have all equipment to hand .


All cleaning products / hand sanitizer will be supplied to each team. Ensure that you have your pack before commencinf training.

Good Practice is to set up a cleaning station which can be accessed one person at a time.
