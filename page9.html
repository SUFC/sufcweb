Skelton United FC are pleased to announce the launch of our new Wildcats Centre
providing Girls only football sessions at our DeBrus playing field

Wildcats football centres  offer girls aged 5-11 a chance to try football for the first time and provide regular opportunities to play.

Sessions take place on a weekly basis and are focused on helping girls make friends, have fun and be active through football.

Unfortunately our sessions have been postponed due to the current Coronavirus situation. We will keep the situation under review and will be back as soon as it is safe to do so.

In the meantime, please see below for some great ideas to help your Wildcat to keep busy during the lockdown
